What is vector

Vector is a list of numbers. which looks like,

Here we consider vector as a point or an arrow start from origin in n Dimension. n is depands on the length of vector. The first number tell us how far it parallel to x-axis. The second tell us how far it parallel to y-axis. When we add whose two vectors together. We move the second vector to the tail of the first vector.

So, the question is why adding proforms like that.

The first matrix tells us move 1 step right then move 2 steps up. The second metrix tells us to move 3 steps right then move 1 step down. And the add operation tells us we can separate those movement. Firstly, we do the operation on the x-axis then on the y-axis. We move 1 step right then 3 steps right which equal to move 4 steps right. We do the same thing on y-axis.
